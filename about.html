What we are

A corporate organisation created to bridge the gap between producers, retailers and buyers.

AgroLead was founded on the innovation of four minds to solve the scaling gap between farmers and consumers(users of farm produce).
Its was observed that farm produce lie in waste often because there was no link for someone to purchase because there is always someone who needs them.

We have a team of committed personalities who are constantly ready to attend to transactions from the buying or selling party.
Our contact is also always ready for enquiries on how to join the platform. DO not hesitate to call or send a mail to agroleadconnect@gmail.com.
